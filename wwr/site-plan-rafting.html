Overview

Purpose

This website aims to deliver a captivating and user-friendly platform for potential customers of a water rafting company. Its primary functions include showcasing diverse rafting experiences, providing clear trip details, enabling seamless online booking, and fostering a sense of excitement that translates to increased conversions.

Audience

Our website is designed for adventure enthusiasts who are interested in water rafting. Who are the target customers? Our target customers are young adults to middle-aged individuals (18-50 years old) who are physically active, enjoy outdoor activities, and are financially capable of indulging in adventure sports. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? They expect to find detailed information about various rafting locations, safety guidelines, equipment details, and booking options for rafting trips. How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? Given the current digital trends, we anticipate that a significant portion of our audience will access our site via mobile devices, although we also expect a fair amount of traffic from desktop and laptop users. Therefore, our website will be designed to be responsive and user-friendly across all devices.
