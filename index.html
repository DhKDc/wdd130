Chile

Chile is an elongated, narrow country that extends along South America's western border, boasting an extensive Pacific coastline spanning over 6,000 kilometers. Its capital city, Santiago, is nestled within a valley embraced by the Andes and Coastal Range mountain chains. At the heart of Santiago lies the Plaza de Armas, a palm-fringed square adorned with the neoclassical Metropolitan Cathedral and the National History Museum. The sprawling Parque Metropolitano, a verdant oasis within the city limits, encompasses swimming pools, a botanical garden, and a zoo.
